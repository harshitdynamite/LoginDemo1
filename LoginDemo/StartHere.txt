Steps to start -:

Open the solution in visual studio.
Hit Ctrl+F5 to start the application -
	this should start the service
	And bring up the browser
	On the browser hit this url- https://localhost:5001/swagger/index.html

Find a lock button at the top right corner, click it
Enter any text in the dialog that comes up
This will do the jwt authentication for your user.
and further api's should be good to go.
